My name is Steve...

Hi! My name is Steve Friedlander. I am a student at the University of Florida, and an aspiring web designer and graphic artist. I am married to my wife, Solita, and a proud dog dad to our energetic and sweet dog, Joey. After 20 years of working in I.T., I decided I wanted to move on to something new. I am currently enrolled as a graduate student at the University of Florida in the M.S. of Web Design and Online Communications program. I have always had a creative side along with an interest in design and aesthetic; whether it has been playing music in past bands or studying art and architecure through my travels. Coincidentally, I love to travel so that I can see, feel, taste and observe as much of the world as possible. I am also fond of hiking, mountain biking, sociology and history.
